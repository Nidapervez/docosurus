---

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI, exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and the physical world. We will delve into the history of AI, from its early theoretical beginnings to the development of embodied agents that can perceive, reason, and act in real-world environments.

### Key Topics:

*   **What is Physical AI?**: Defining the field and its key characteristics.
*   **A Brief History of AI and Robotics**: Tracing the evolution of intelligent machines.
*   **The Embodied Agent**: Understanding the concept of an AI that exists in a physical form.
*   **Sensing and Perception**: How Physical AI systems interpret the world around them.
*   **Actuation and Control**: The mechanisms that allow Physical AI to interact with its environment.
