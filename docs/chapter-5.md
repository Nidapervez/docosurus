This chapter provides a comprehensive overview of humanoid robotics. We will explore the history of humanoid robots, their design and construction, and the challenges of creating machines that mimic human form and function.

### Key Topics:

*   **The History of Humanoid Robots**: From science fiction to reality.
*   **Bipedal Locomotion**: The challenge of walking on two legs.
*   **Human-Robot Interaction**: Designing robots that can work alongside humans.
*   **The Uncanny Valley**: The psychological aspects of humanoid design.
