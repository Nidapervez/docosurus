title: "AI Ethics and Safety"
---

This chapter addresses the ethical and safety considerations of Physical AI. We will discuss the potential risks and benefits of advanced AI and robotics, and explore frameworks for ensuring that these technologies are developed and used responsibly.

### Key Topics:

*   **The Asilomar AI Principles**: A set of guidelines for AI safety research.
*   **Bias in AI**: The problem of algorithmic bias and its societal impact.
*   **The Control Problem**: Ensuring that advanced AI systems remain aligned with human values.
*   **The Future of Work**: The economic and social implications of automation.
