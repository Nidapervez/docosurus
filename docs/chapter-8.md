This chapter showcases the wide range of applications for Physical AI. We will explore how these technologies are being used in fields such as manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, and exploration.

### Key Topics:

*   **Robotics in Manufacturing**: The automated factory of the future.
*   **Surgical Robots**: Assisting surgeons in the operating room.
*   **Autonomous Drones**: From package delivery to disaster response.
*   **Space Exploration**: Robots on other worlds.
