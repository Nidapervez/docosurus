title: "The Future of Physical AI"
---

This chapter looks to the future of Physical AI. We will discuss the latest research trends and speculate on the long-term impact of these technologies on society.

### Key Topics:

*   **Soft Robotics**: Robots made from flexible materials.
*   **Swarm Robotics**: The power of collective intelligence.
*   **Artificial General Intelligence**: The quest for human-level AI.
*   **The Singularity**: The hypothetical point in time at which technological growth becomes uncontrollable and irreversible.
